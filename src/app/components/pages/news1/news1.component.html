MEDALS FOR CILACAP DISTRICT FROM NASPO AND I2ASPO

CILACAP - Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) in collaboration with the Indonesian Young Scientist Association (IYSA) organized a national level competition, namely the National Applied Science Olympiad (NASPO) which was attended by 232 teams of students and students throughout Indonesia and an international level competition entitled Indonesia International Applied Science Olympiad (I2ASPO) which was attended by 300 teams of students and students from various countries, including Indonesia, Turkey, Thailand, Vietnam, Puerto Rico, Philippines, United Arab Emirates, Kazakhstan, Romania, Singapore, Iran, Malaysia, and Hong Kong.


The NASPO and I2ASPO competitions were held hybridly at the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS) on December 19-21, 2023. In the NASPO competition, the SMA Negeri 1 Maos team consisting of Cahaya Eli Prasetya Ningrum, Alan Firid Aryadi, Aprilia Aris Kristianto, Bagus Sukma Rakhmanto, and Nur Fadilah won the Gold Medal with their work "Javanese Alphabet Quartet Card Game as Learning Media to Improve Javanese Letter Reading Ability."


In their research, the SMA Negeri 1 Maos team created a quartet card game called Kartu Beja to help students in mastering Javanese script reading and writing.


At the international I2ASPO event, the SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap team consisting of Nasywa Roesviana Putri, Mouzya Syalwa Khoirunnisa, Vinon Apip Pranata, Zahrana Nur Azizah, and Zahro Wahyu Rohmani won the Gold Medal with their research entitled "The Discourse on Restricting Marriage for PLHIV in Cilacap Regency."


In their research, the team from SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap explored the discourse on restricting marriage for PLHIV in Cilacap Regency. Based on a long discussion, the discourse was finally not realized because PLHIV are also humans and HIV transmission to their spouses and children can be prevented through medical interventions.


Meanwhile, the SMA Negeri 1 Maos team consisting of Elyne Kusuma Mahardika, Annisa Nurus Shillah, Cika Ridhaputrimardani, Rofi Arif Robbani, and Talitha Aulia Zuriah Salsabila won the Silver Medal with the research title "Optimizing The AquaCrop Model for Predicting Agricultural Yields Using Agroclimatic Caping."


The research of SMA Negeri 1 Maos team is an effort to make the accuracy of AquaCrop application with Agroclimatic Caping to predict agricultural yields. Head of SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap Dra. Masripah, M.M.Pd was proud of her students' achievements in the international arena. "Alhamdulillah, cool, a proud achievement, hopefully it can inspire other students," said Masripah.


The same thing was conveyed by the Head of SMA Negeri 1 Maos Amin, S.Pd., M.Pd. he felt happy and proud of the achievements obtained by his students in the NASPO and I2ASPO events. He hopes that these achievements can motivate his friends to continue working and achieving. "Alhamdulillah, this is a gift for the end of 2023 and an extraordinary and proud achievement from Cilacap students in national and international science and technology competitions. Congratulations and success always for the Champions!", said Amin. (wd/kominfo)
