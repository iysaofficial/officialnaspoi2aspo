SMA Muhammadiyah 1 Bantul wins NASPO and I²ASPO ITS championships

SURABAYA, Suara Muhammadiyah - Taking place at the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS) Surabaya on December 19-22, 2023, SMA Muhammadiyah 1 Bantul Yogyakarta participated in national and international research competitions at ITS Surabaya in the Indonesia International Applied Science Project Olympiad (I²ASPO) and National Applied Science Project Olympiad (NASPO) competitions.


The two concurrent events organized by IYSA (Indonesian Young Scientist Association) in collaboration with the Department of Actuarial FSAD ITS Surabaya were attended by 38 NASPO 2023 offline competition teams from all over Indonesia, 232 I2ASPO offline competition teams from 17 delegations from various countries such as: Indonesia, Turkey, Taiwan, Vietnam, South Aftika, Thailand, Kazakhstan, Malaysia and several countries in Asia.


Sri Widyastuti, S.Pd, teacher and supervisor of SMA MUH 1 Bantul students said that in this event they sent 2 teams, Team 1 (International) of 4 students: Vonny Rizanti Syairtha (Leader), Aisyah Naila Basuki, Anindya Putri Salsabila, Shafa Salsabila and team 2 of 5 students namely; Team 2 (National) Madinah Raudiah, (Leader), Alfara Azeta Hyadi, Alya Adz Dzikra Surya K. N, Alifah Nur Fatimah, and Djenar Kinanti Cikal Rinakit.


At the international I2ASPO event, the SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap team consisting of Nasywa Roesviana Putri, Mouzya Syalwa Khoirunnisa, Vinon Apip Pranata, Zahrana Nur Azizah, and Zahro Wahyu Rohmani won the Gold Medal with their research entitled "The Discourse on Restricting Marriage for PLHIV in Cilacap Regency."


In these 2 competitions, SMA MUH 1 Bantul, Team 1 (International) participated in the waste treatment category with the title Use of Static Electricity to Reduce Gas Emission in Plastic Waste Burning Machines and Team 2 (National) energy category with the research title Candlenut Fruit Extract and Tea as Anti-dandruff Serum for Muslim Women Hijab Users.


From these two competitions, SMA MUH 1 Bantul Yogyakarta won the championship, Team 1 with leader Vonny Rizanti Syairtha won the gold medal and Team 2 with leader Madinah Raudiah won the silver medal and special award said Sri Widyastuti, S.Pd.


Vonny Rizanti Syairtha and Madinah Raudiah, students of SMA MUH 1 Bantul both expressed their gratitude for the support and prayers from parents, supervising teachers, SMA MUH 1 Bantul leaders, Dikdasmen PDM Bantul, Dikdasmen PWM DIY and all those who have helped.


Through the I²ASPO ESP Team competition, we can get a Gold Medal, we are very happy after processing together for the creation of electrostatic electricity, now the war is over and we did it, we are also re-motivated to continue further research and provide benefits in the future and honestly did not expect the first try we got that special award and silver medal, we are really grateful for that. Thank you for all the prayers and support given to us, ESP Team and Milka Team.
