SMAN 1 Maos and SMAN 2 Cilacap Make Achievements

Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) in collaboration with the Indonesian Young Scientist Association (IYSA) has successfully organized two prestigious competitions. First, the National Applied Science Olympiad (NASPO), a national level competition attended by 232 teams of students and students from all over Indonesia. In addition, ITS also hosted an international level competition, the Indonesia International Applied Science Olympiad (I2ASPO), which was attended by 300 teams of students from various countries. Participants came from Indonesia itself, as well as countries such as Turkey, Thailand, Vietnam, Puerto Rico, the Philippines, the United Arab Emirates, Kazakhstan, Romania, Singapore, Iran, Malaysia and Hong Kong. The successful organization of these two competitions represents a remarkable achievement in creating a competitive platform for young scientists to share their knowledge and hone their skills in applied sciences.


On December 19-21, 2023, ITS hosted the NASPO and I2ASPO Competitions in a hybrid format. One notable achievement in the NASPO competition came from the SMA Negeri 1 Maos team. The team, consisting of Cahaya Eli Prasetya Ningrum, Alan Firid Aryadi, Aprilia Aris Kristianto, Bagus Sukma Rakhmanto, and Nur Fadilah, won a gold medal. This achievement was obtained thanks to their work entitled "Javanese Alphabet Quartet Card Game as a Learning Media to Improve the Ability to Read Javanese Letters".


The SMA Negeri 1 Maos team won the gold medal in NASPO 2023 with their latest research. In an effort to help students master Javanese script reading and writing, the team created Kartu Beja, an innovative quartet card game. Kartu Beja is designed as an effective learning tool to strengthen Javanese reading and writing skills among students.


In addition to the NASPO 2023 gold medal, SMA Negeri 1 Maos also won a silver medal through Elyne Kusuma Mahardika, Annisa Nurus Shillah, Cika Ridhaputrimardani, Rofi Arif Robbani, and Talitha Aulia Zuriah Salsabila. They won the silver medal with their research entitled "Optimizing The AquaCrop Model for Predicting Agricultural Yields Using Agroclimatic Caping."


The research team of SMA Negeri 1 Maos made a significant achievement by trying to improve the accuracy of the AquaCrop application through the application of Agroclimate Caping to predict agricultural yields. This achievement not only gives pride to their school but also becomes a meaningful contribution in the development of agricultural science and technology.


SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap At the I2ASPO international competition, the SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap team also recorded brilliant achievements. The team members, Nasywa Roesviana Putri, Mouzya Syalwa Khoirunnisa, Vinon Apip Pranata, Zahrana Nur Azizah, and Zahro Wahyu Rohmani, won a gold medal with their research entitled "The Discourse on Restricting Marriage for PLHIV in Cilacap Regency".


The team from SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap explored the discourse on restricting marriage for PLHIV in Cilacap Regency. Through a long discussion, it was finally decided that the discourse would not be realized. This decision was based on the understanding that PLWHA are human beings, and HIV transmission to their spouses and children can be prevented through appropriate medical intervention.


Dra. Masripah, M.M.Pd, Head of SMA Negeri 2 Cilacap, expressed her joy at the brilliant achievements of her students in the international arena. "Alhamdulillah... cool, a proud achievement. Hopefully it can inspire other students," she said proudly. He hopes that the achievements in NASPO and I2ASPO can motivate students to continue to excel.
