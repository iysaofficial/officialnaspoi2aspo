AAL Cadets Win Gold and Bronze Medals in I2ASPO and NASPO Events

At the end of 2021, Naval Academy (AAL) cadets made history by winning Gold and Bronze Medals at the Indonesia International Applied Science Project Olympiad (I2ASPO) and National Applied Science Project Olympiad (NASPO) Scientific Writing Competition (LKTI) held by the Indonesia Young Scientist Association (IYSA) in collaboration with the Actuarial Department of the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology (ITS) Surabaya.


This was said by the Head of the Department of Science and Technology (Kadepiptek) AAL, Marine Colonel (KH) Said Masykur, representing the Governor of AAL, Maj. Gen. TNI (Mar) Nur Alamsyah at the Galasa Building, Depiptek, AAL Bumimoro Satrian Building, Surabaya, Wednesday (15/12/2021).


According to Kadepiptek, this LKTI competition activity was carried out offline and online from mid-November 2021 until the judging session which was held on December 12, 2021. AAL continued, sending two teams to this event. The I2ASPO AAL team consists of Sermadatar (P) Alfan Prasurya Adhipatiyan, Sermadatar (T) Regel Satria Mardika, Sermadatar (E) Gilang Pratama Bahari, Sermadatar (S) Daffa Agusta, and Sermadatar (M) Ryan Ardimas.


Meanwhile, the NASPO Team was filled with Sermadatar (P) Resa Ridwan Pindriansyah, Sermadatar (T) M. Hafish Zaki Pratama, Sermadatar (E) Indana Zulfa, Sermadatar (S) Zenita Sanadila, and Sermadatar (M) I Made Indrayana Wibaya. These two teams were accompanied by AAL Cadet as Supervisor, Lieutenant Colonel (KH/W) Mostien Adi Puji as coordinator with four supervisors, namely, Lieutenant Colonel (T) Adiyus Kurnia Zain, Marine Major (T) Sonnie Rakhmawan, PNS Evi Martiningsih, and PNS Risa Apriani.


Since the pandemic is not over, the AAL Team participated in the I2ASPO and NASPO competitions online by sending an LKTI script entitled "IAF (Incenerator Air Filter) as a Standardization of Smoke Waste Control on Ships To Preserve The Environment" or "IAF (Incenerator Air Filter) As a Standardization of Smoke Waste Control On Ships To Preserve The Environment".


"Alhamdulillah, I2ASPO received three awards at once, namely, the Gold Medal for the Weste Treatment Category at the University Level, then Free registration to BUCA IMSEF Turkey 2022 (Online) and the IYSA Reward award in the form of coaching funds as the best team," concluded Said. (HJR)
